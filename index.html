About EMS

We attended school in Kenya at a time when preparing results
of national examinations took months. The results for exams
done in November used to be released in March the next year.
The government of Kenya shifted to a more rushed form of exams
marking and results preparation in 2016. This saw an historic
drop in performance amongst student. In the recent days, a
competence based curriculum has been adopted. This curriculum
is broader in evaluation terms and thus more demanding from
teachers.
How about we give teachers the power to only focus on keying in
the results of the students and subjects they handle and automate
everything else. This way we'll not only get all teachers involved
in the preparation of the results but also eliminate the need for
teachers to travel to examination marking centers.
The system would work in this simple steps:

  1. A .csv template is provided to all teachers for capturing results.
  2. Teacher enters grades and scores of students in subject of interest.
  3. File entries are captured by system and saved to db in real-time.
  4. Teacher creates custom dashboards and add charts with data they
    wish to analyse.
